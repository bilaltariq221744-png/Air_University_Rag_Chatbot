# Academics Department
Fostering innovation through an enriching academic ecosystem
## Our Motto
“Empowering minds through a dynamic educational ecosystem that ensures a pleasant and conducive learning and workplace environment for students and faculty by embracing a student-centric approach without compromising on the quality of education”. 
## Introduction
The Academics Department is Air University's academic hub. It coordinates and integrates various academic operations across the university, its Campuses, Constituent Units, and Affiliated Colleges, ultimately aiming to enhance the quality of education. Its core operations include curriculum development, instructional delivery, formulation of academic rules and regulations, and interpretation of academic policies in line with those of the university and regulatory bodies. The office is also responsible for the accreditation of programs by relevant councils and obtaining No Objection Certificates from HEC for starting new programs. 
In addition to its primary duties, this office provides academic support to other core operations of the university, such as exams, registration, admissions, automation, the quality enhancement cell, and graduate studies. The office facilitates the implementation of market-driven academic programs, focus on improving the skill set of graduates, contribute to the country's economic development, belong to emerging technological domains, and encourage innovation and creativity. Director Academics also oversees the AU Central Library.
