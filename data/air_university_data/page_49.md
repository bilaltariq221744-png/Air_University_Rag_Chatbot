Dean's Message 
The Air University School of Management (AUSOM), as an academic unit of Air University, has carved a distinct place for itself amongst the top national business schools. Built purposefully for imparting learning through new and innovative methods of teaching, the environment of the department has been further galvanized through the case-based method of teaching. This provides an excellent and enabling environment for the students to hone in their management skills.
It is this unique combination of technology integration, a customized learning structure, and a faculty par excellence that makes AUSOM an exceptionally unparalleled learning Institution. Strong industry-academia linkage established by the School through a continuous process of interaction with corporate industry leaders in workshops, seminars, lectures and other extracurricular events has placed AUSOM at a uniquely advantageous position. With a distinctive competitive advantage and an enthusiastic and inspired student body produced by the School, a large number of leading firms and corporate entities take part in Air University campus recruitments. 
AUSOM alumni is serving at different leadership positions in various private, public, multinational, government, profit and nonprofit organizations. This distinctive position attained by AUSOM would not have been possible without the dedication and untiring efforts of a professional team. The students, faculty members, administrative staff, and the University management cohesively form the closely aligned core of this team. Together we ensure a culture of continuous learning, flexibility, friendliness, and discipline.
