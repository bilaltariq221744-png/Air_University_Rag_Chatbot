# A Platform For Graduate Students' Research
#### Welcome to the webpage of AU JoGR an online journal providing a platform for graduate students to present their research to write high quality papers and to form linkages with researchers of similar interests.
#### Papers are invited in all disciplines offered at Air University including Sciences and Engineering, the Social Sciences (Humanities, Education and Psychology), Strategic Studies, Management and Finance.
#### To submit your work, please see the [Word Template ](https://au.edu.pk/Pages/Graduate_Studies/documents/Standardized_Thesis_Template/AUJoGRTemplate.docx)for submission of your Manuscript.
