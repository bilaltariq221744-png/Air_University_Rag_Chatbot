###  [ **KHARIAN CAMPUS** ](https://kc.au.edu.pk/)
Established in 2024, Air University Kharian Campus stands as a symbol of academic growth and innovation. The modern, purpose-built campus provides a vibrant learning environment where dedicated faculty guide students to achieve academic excellence and professional success. It currently offers undergraduate programs in Management Sciences (BBA and BS Accounting & Finance) and Computing (BS Computer Science, BS Cyber Security, and BS Artificial Intelligence), along with MS programs in Management and Computer Science. The campus remains committed to advancing research, innovation, and quality education in the region.

###  [ **Aerospace & Aviation Campus Kamra** ](https://aack.au.edu.pk/)
The Campus is a great model of Academia-Industry-R&D linkage in Pakistan due to proximity of AU Kamra, PAC and NASTP Kamra. AU Kamra, as vital organ of National Aerospace City Kamra, has all the potential to contribute significantly towards muchdesired academia industry collaboration. The Campus has about 1200 enrolled students and currently running a total of 21 degree programs at undergraduate, graduate and postgraduate levels in engineering/non-engineering disciplines. The Campus boasts of Aerospace Engineering and Avionics Engineering as its niche disciplines. The campus has specialized faculty available in all Departments as per HEC requirements.
