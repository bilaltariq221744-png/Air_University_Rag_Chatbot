## Welcome to International Cooperation Office
Air University
The International Cooperation Office (ICO), established in 2008, plays a vital role in promoting the internationalization of higher education, research, and innovation at Air University. Through global partnerships, exchange programs, collaborative research, and international conferences, the ICO continues to strengthen AU’s global presence and academic linkages. The management at Air University also realizes that International educational cooperation is a mean for enhancing and fostering an international atmosphere on the university’s campuses, thus promoting personal growth and understanding of global responsibility among university members.
