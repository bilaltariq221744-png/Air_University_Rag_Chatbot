Dean Message 
  

FCAI is one of the leading and fastest-growing computing Faculties in Pakistan due to its quality academic, research and enterprise activities. In 2016, FCAI was a computer science department with two-degree programmes, 500 students, and 2 research groups. In 2019, the department progressed to Faculty of Computing & Artificial Intelligence with three different Departments. These are Department of Computer Science, Creative Technologies and Cyber Security which conduct 15-degree programmes, and have 10 research groups, 3 technical student societies, and 1500 students. All new academic programmes have been started with an aim to produce a skilful workforce which can contribute to international computing markets. It is very encouraging to highlight that FCAI students’ job placements and success in winning International scholarships have increased substantially in recent years. 
FCAI faculty members are rated among world’s top 2% researchers and are actively engaged in bringing external research grants. Our highest priority is to prepare the next generation of scientists through our MS and PhD programmes to solve computing domain challenges, while providing the training our students need to compete and excel in the field. In future, we plan to establish leading national research labs.
