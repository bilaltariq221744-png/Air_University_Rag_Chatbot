###  [**Karachi Campus**](https://khi.au.edu.pk/)
Air University has its footprints in Karachi through Fazaia Ruth Pfau Medical College (FRPMC), a consituent insittution of Air University, Islamabad. The College was established in 2019 at PAF Base Faisal. Named after the renowned humanitarian Dr. Ruth Pfau, the college offers a modern medical education aligned with national standards. With advanced facilities and strong clinical affiliations, FRPMC is committed to producing skilled and compassionate healthcare professionals.

Air University Kharian Campus was established as part of the university's expansion efforts and officially opened around early 2024. It features modern facilities and a focus on research collaboration through proximity to the National Aerospace Science and Technology Park, aiming to boost educational opportunities and economic development in the Kharian area.

### [**Air University H-11 Campus**](https://au.edu.pk/)
Air University’s new state-of-the-art H-11 Campus is set to become operational from the Fall 2025 semester. Designed to meet modern academic and research needs, the campus will offer BS, MS, and PhD programs in Business Studies, Social Sciences, and Aerospace & Strategic Studies. With a capacity to accommodate over 7,000 students, the purpose-built facility reflects the university’s commitment to excellence in education and innovation. This milestone marks a significant expanding Air University’s academic footprint in the capital.

###  [**Bahu Campus, Shorkot Cantt**](https://sk.au.edu.pk/)
Air University expands its legacy of excellence to Central Punjab with the Future Projects of its Bahu Campus Shorkot Cantt in 2026. Strategically positioned to serve the Faisalabad, Jhang, and Sahiwal regions, the campus aims to bridge the educational gap in underserved areas through a mix of accredited and emerging programs aligned with technological growth and market demands. By launching flagship programs and fostering a niche for innovation and applied learning, the campus strengthens AU’s brand presence in the region. Ranked No. 2 in Pakistan and among the 600–800 universities globally by Times Higher Education (THE), Air University continues to empower Pakistani minds for global leadership in the innovation economy through its state-of-the-art infrastructure, modern laboratories, and highly qualified faculty.
