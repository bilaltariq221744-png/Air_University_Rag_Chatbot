###  [**AU Multan Campus**](http://aumc.edu.pk/)
Air University Multan Campus is committed to delivering excellence in higher education and research. The campus offers a dynamic learning environment that integrates academic rigor with practical relevance, preparing students to meet the evolving demands of the global marketplace. With a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty, it emphasizes innovation, critical thinking, and professional competence. Equipped with state-of-the-art classrooms, advanced laboratories, and cutting-edge teaching technologies, the campus provides an inspiring academic setting that nurtures creativity and lifelong learning. It continues to play a pivotal role in the socioeconomic development of the region by fostering knowledge, leadership, and innovation.

###  [**Aerospace & Aviation Campus Kamra**](https://aack.au.edu.pk/)
The Campus is a great model of Academia-Industry-R&D linkage in Pakistan due to proximity of AU Kamra, PAC and NASTP Kamra. AU Kamra, as vital organ of National Aerospace City Kamra, has all the potential to contribute significantly towards muchdesired academia industry collaboration. The Campus has about 1200 enrolled students and currently running a total of 21 degree programs at undergraduate, graduate and postgraduate levels in engineering/non-engineering disciplines. The Campus boasts of Aerospace Engineering and Avionics Engineering as its niche disciplines. The campus has specialized faculty available in all Departments as per HEC requirements.
